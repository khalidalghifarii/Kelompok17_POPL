About Our Food & Restaurant

Welcome to SPICYO, Banda Aceh's beloved restaurant where we bring the warmth of homemade cooking to every plate. Our passion for food goes beyond just serving meals; we are committed to creating an experience that feels like home. At SPICYO, we believe that great food starts with the finest, freshest ingredients, carefully selected to craft dishes that are both flavorful and wholesome.

Best Food

If you're craving a savory delight at SPICYO, don't miss out on our Best Food dish. Featuring perfectly grilled salmon fillets glazed with a rich soy-based sauce, and topped with fresh scallions and toasted sesame seeds, this dish offers a delicious blend of flavors and textures. It's a must-try for seafood lovers looking for a refined yet comforting meal, bursting with umami in every bite.

Spicy Barger

A bold twist on the classic burger, our Spicy Barger packs a flavorful punch! Juicy beef patty seasoned with a special blend of spices, topped with fresh lettuce, tomatoes, and all nestled between soft, toasted buns. This burger is served with a generous side of crispy fries. Perfect for those who love a little heat in their meals!

Egg & Tosh

A hearty breakfast option, the Egg & Tosh is a delightful combination of perfectly scrambled eggs and savory toast. Garnished with herbs and served with a side of crispy bacon or sausage, this dish is a wholesome way to start your day or enjoy as a light yet satisfying meal at any time. A true comfort food with a homemade touch.

Pizza

Our homemade Pizza features a thin, crispy crust topped with a rich tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella, and your choice of premium toppings. Whether you're in the mood for a classic Margherita or a meat lover's delight, each pizza is made fresh to order and baked to perfection. A slice of heaven in every bite!

Our Chef

Peter Parker

Peter Parker is a highly skilled chef at SPICYO, a renowned restaurant in Banda Aceh known for its delicious homemade cuisine. With years of experience and a passion for culinary excellence, Peter has mastered the art of blending flavors and crafting dishes that leave a lasting impression on every diner. His creativity in the kitchen and dedication to using fresh, local ingredients have earned SPICYO its reputation as a go-to spot for authentic, heartwarming meals. Peter’s philosophy is simple yet profound: "Cooking is not just about feeding the body; it’s about nourishing the soul with every bite." His dishes reflect this belief, making every visit to SPICYO a truly memorable experience.
